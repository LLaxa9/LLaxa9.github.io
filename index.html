Bio

Read time: 5 minutes. Gazing at paintings: inestimable.

Born in 1956 in Romania, the artist’s journey is a testament to resilience and passion. His diverse career path includes roles as an accountant, administrator, naval pilot officer, canteen head, and guardsman. These experiences have shaped his unique perspective and drive to push himself and inspire others to achieve their best.

After retirement due to illness,his passion for creativity never waned. He attended a painting course at "Dunarea de Jos" Cultural Center’s Art School, where he honed his artistic skills.

Today, his paintings have captivated art aficionados in a couple European countries, reflecting his rich life experiences and unwavering passion for art.

Artist statement

Oil painting allows me to unleash my raw emotions onto the canvas, 17 years spent outdoors in the Danube Delta, and my experience as a sailor reaching the vibrant streets of India to the lush landscapes of Brazil have enriched my palette with diverse colours and textures. Through continuous experimentation and speedy execution I strive to capture the essence of my inner world and display nature’s grace.

Inspiration and Influences

Art is a profound expression of the human experience, capturing emotions, stories, and moments in time. The journey of creation can be fueled by a plethora of inspirations, while nature and family often serve as the bedrock of artistic motivation, there are unique experiences and personalities that challenged and nurtured my growth.

Influential Personalities

My most recent mentor, Prof. Stefan Olimpia, has been guiding my studies for three years. She has helped me see colors in a new light, encouraged experimentation, and provided constructive criticism. Her passion for art and teaching ignites a similar passion in her students, fostering a lifelong love for creativity.

During my high school years, the art teacher, Prof. Nicolae Einhorn, played a crucial role in my development. He refined my technical drawing skills, introduced me to the medium of glass icons, and encouraged me to continue pursuing art.

Having Pavel Botezatu as a friend since childhood has been a source of great inspiration. His presence served as a concrete illustration of achievable goals, providing mentorship, assistance, and a peek into the professional artist’s world. His influence has given me valuable perspectives on tackling landscape art.

Studio Transformation Underway

We are excited to announce that we are currently under construction, transforming our garage into a studio dedicated to creating frames and displaying artwork.

This new space will provide a welcomming and inspiring environment for artwork.

Transformations for the Studio:

  • Redirected old piping to optimize the layout.

  • Installed a new parquet floor for a polished look.

Precision and Elegance: The CASSESE CS55M Foot Operated Guillotine

We have new capabilities to manufacture frames for paintings, we dispose of a CASSESE CS55M Foot Operated Guillotine.

It is a machine designed to meet the highest standards in frame manufacturing. This guillotine is engineered for precision and efficiency, making it an essential tool for achieving frames at a professional level.
